# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be useful and be kind. This is a community of operators helping each other run companies better with AI. Treat people the way you'd want a stranger to treat you when you're stuck at 2am debugging a webhook.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive, especially in disagreement.
- Assume good intent. Most people here are learning.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Help newcomers. Everyone was at chapter 00 once.
- Keep private data private — yours and everyone else's.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination of any kind.
- Publishing someone else's private information.
- Spam, self-promotion disguised as contribution, or vendor pitches.
- Deliberately bad-faith arguments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, and contributions that violate this code, and may block repeat offenders. To report a problem, email **hello@usecompai.com**.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
