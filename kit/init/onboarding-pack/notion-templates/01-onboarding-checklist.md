# {BRAND} AI Swarm — Employee Onboarding Checklist

*Import this into Notion as a template. Replace `{BRAND}` and `{founder}` with your values.*

Welcome to {BRAND}'s AI swarm. This onboarding gets you from "never touched an AI agent" to "working fluently with your brand's Claude setup" in under 2 hours across your first week.

There are no certifications, no training videos, no LMS. The brain + your me.md + a daily habit of `/learn` is the full curriculum.

---

## Day 1 (45 min — must finish today)

- [ ] **Step 1 — Install Claude Desktop** (5 min)
  - Mac: download from claude.ai/download
  - Windows: download from claude.ai/download
  - Linux: use the web app or AppImage
- [ ] **Step 2 — Confirm subscription** (2 min)
  - {BRAND} covers Claude Pro (€20/mo). Check with {founder} that yours is activated.
- [ ] **Step 3 — Connect to {BRAND}'s swarm** (10 min)
  - {founder} will send you a message with a one-line `curl` command
  - Paste it into your terminal (Mac / Linux) or PowerShell (Windows)
  - It installs Node.js if you don't have it, writes the Claude Desktop config, and asks for your API key
  - Your API key was generated by {founder} specifically for you — it's how the brain knows it's you
- [ ] **Step 4 — Verify connection** (3 min)
  - Quit and reopen Claude Desktop
  - Click the tools icon (bottom right of chat input)
  - You should see "{BRAND}" with ~11 tools listed
  - Ask Claude: *"What tools do you have access to?"* — it should list brain_query, brain_read, shopify_query, etc.
- [ ] **Step 5 — Paste the custom instruction** (5 min)
  - Open Claude Desktop → Settings → Profile → "What should Claude know about you?"
  - Paste the block from `custom-instruction.md` (sent to you separately)
  - This primes every chat with your brand's operational contract
- [ ] **Step 6 — Create your me.md** (15 min)
  - In a new chat, type: *"Run the me-md-interview skill. My name is {your-name-lowercase}"*
  - Answer 6-8 questions conversationally
  - Claude writes your profile to the brain
  - Verify: in a fresh chat, ask *"read my me.md"* — it should come back
- [ ] **Step 7 — First real task** (5 min)
  - Pick one question you'd normally ask a colleague
  - Ask Claude instead — let it `brain_query` first, then answer
  - Note how long the answer took vs a colleague would have taken

## Week 1 (1h spread across 5 days)

- [ ] **Day 2 — First ticket/report using Claude**
  - Whatever your first daily task is, do it with Claude in the loop
  - Cut-paste results, don't overthink it
- [ ] **Day 3 — Explore one integration**
  - You have access to Shopify, Klaviyo, the accounting system, etc. Pick one you know.
  - Ask Claude *"what can you tell me about our last 30 days of {the thing you know}?"*
  - Cross-check 3 numbers it gives you against the source tool
- [ ] **Day 4 — First `/learn`**
  - At the end of your day, type `/learn`
  - Claude proposes 1-3 things to save to the brain
  - Accept or reject — there's no wrong answer
- [ ] **Day 5 — Share with the team**
  - In your team chat (Slack / Teams / whatever), share one thing Claude did for you this week that saved >15 min
  - If another teammate hasn't done onboarding yet, show them how

## Help & troubleshooting

- **Claude Desktop doesn't show the brand's tools:** re-run the team-join command, quit & reopen Claude Desktop.
- **Tool calls return errors:** your API key might be revoked or expired. Ask {founder} for a new one via `operai-init key create`.
- **brain_query returns nothing useful:** the brain might not have been populated yet on that topic — ask a colleague to write a doc, or write one yourself.
- **Something is confusing:** DM {founder} or post in #{BRAND}-ai. Not figuring it out alone is part of the protocol.
